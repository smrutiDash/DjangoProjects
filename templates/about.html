About Us

HireRise is based in Navi Mumbai and catering to MSMEs throughout the country. We have been in practice for about a decade. The ingenious manpower solutions, associating human potential to the power of enterprises. Our main objective is client satisfaction and offering recruiting solutions all over India, by providing accurate assessment, talent mapping, and wide networking across all businesses. Our orientation and approach are very different from the traditional placement agencies. Unlike the current placement agencies’ work scope which is myopic and narrow, we typically work as an extended recruitment partner for you.

We have a ‘dedicated hiring approach’ model with a strong belief that a Talented workforce can make a BIG impact on the success of MSME.

We work directly with business owners and help them recruit strong talent. The success of the Indian economy majorly depends on the success of MSMEs. Since MSME has few employees, more depends on their individual performances. The employee’s performance will determine the organization’s performance as a whole. The best option would be to find candidates who are both high performers and high 'cultural fit'. We guide our partner Business owners to change their approach from "filling of vacancy" to "continuous talent scouting" by analyzing the work culture along with the job vacancies and match them with the best talent fit.
